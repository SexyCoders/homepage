About Us

The SexyCoders team was founded by Sotirios Anagnostopoulos from Greece in 2020 with a vision: becoming a community for code-sharing and cooperation in projects!

Since then, SexyCoders members have generated millions of lines of code for numerous applications and libraries.

The core team is lead by Mr. Anagnostopoulos and is primarily focusing on modern IoT solutions and dynamic, distributed web apps. But our democratic, open source ideals remain:

ANYONE can contribute code to our familly, even if they do not ever wish to hit the market!

How do i join SexyCoders?

To join our team you simply have to contribute code to our projects! Then, you can officially be called a very Sexy coder!

Can i join the core team?

Our commercial core team only consists of the necessary people to run the projects, since we believe in the community!

This means that if you have an idea for one of our commercial apps, please reach out and we will discuss the way to move forward and include it.
